Our Vision

Project Astraeus is determined to build a network for youth to become the next generation's leaders in innovation, while giving them the opportunity to learn through team projects aimed at fixing local issues.

We are going to do this through:

  • A network of communities seeking local issues, employing entrepreneurial spirit.
  • With seed funding, mentorship, and physical resources, to aid students make.
  • Insightful workshops, keynote lectures, and creative kit projects at community centres.

Our Structure

A network of student-led chapters dedicated to researching and implementing solutions for global and local issues, including accessibility, mental and physical health, environmental concerns, and other prevalent challenges identified by students.
